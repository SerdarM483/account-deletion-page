Account Deletion Request

To delete your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the mobile app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the action

Data Deletion

When you delete your account, all your personal data including your email and profile information will be permanently deleted.

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for backup and legal purposes.
