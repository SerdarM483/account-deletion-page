Account Deletion Request

This page explains how to delete your account for the mobile application.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the mobile app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the action

What data is deleted?

• Email address
• User profile information
• App activity and related data

Data retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for security, backup, and legal obligations. After this period, all remaining data is permanently removed.

App Information

This page is the official data deletion page for the application published on Google Play.
